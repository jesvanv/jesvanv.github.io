Overview

Lesson 1.3 Systems, Experiments, and Models

In this lesson, students learn about systems and the variables that affect them. Students also learn how models can be used to describe systems. Connections are made between science and technology. The engineering cycle is introduced as a way to solve problems.

TEKS

• Knowledge: 6.3.B: Use models to represent aspects of the natural world such as a model of Earth’s layers

Learning Goals

By the end of the lesson, students will be able to...

• Define a system and its variables.

• Contrast different kinds of models.
